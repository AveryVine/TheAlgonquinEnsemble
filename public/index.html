THE ALGONQUIN ENSEMBLE

Who We Are

The Algonquin Ensemble writes & performs music inspired by great Canadian art. The members of The Algonquin Ensemble are: Kathryn Briggs, piano; John Geggie, double bass; Lisa Moody, viola; Laura Nerenberg, violin; Margaret Tobolowska, cello & Terry Tufts, Manzer Palette and assorted fretted instruments. The group is an eclectic mix, merging classical, folk and jazz backgrounds into a magnificent tapestry of musical landscapes.
